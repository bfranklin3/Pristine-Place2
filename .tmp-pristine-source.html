The Jewel of Spring Hill

Welcome to Pristine Place

Pristine Place is a private, gated neighborhood designed to offer a safe, serene, and welcoming environment for our residents. Our HOA is dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for all residents by maintaining the beauty, security, and harmony of our community.

We invite you to discover why Pristine Place is more than just a neighborhood—it’s a place to call home.

Community Highlights

Our neighborhood offers exceptional amenities and benefits for residents.

  • 24/7 Gated Security

    Experience peace of mind in our private, gated community with controlled access for residents, guests, and contractors. Our security features help ensure a safe and serene environment for everyone.

  • Neighborhood Amenities

    Relax and connect in our beautifully maintained wooded and recreational areas. Enjoy an elegant clubhouse, sparkling pool, modern fitness center, tennis courts, pickleball/basketball areas, and a fun playground for children.

  • Convenient Location

    Situated near key landmarks, schools, shopping centers, and highways for easy access to local amenities. Located just off the Suncoast Parkway, it is convenient to the vibrant Tampa Bay area and all its shopping, cultural and recreational attractions.

For Visitors and Contractors

All visitors and contractors must enter through the Minnie Drive Gate, located on St. Ives Blvd at Minnie Dr.

Upon arrival, use the call box to contact the resident you are visiting. Once permission is granted, you will be allowed entry. Please note that contractors may need to provide identification or prior approval from the resident they are working for. 

 

  • Entry Requirements

    Bar Codes, Fobs, and gate codes are required for entry at our gates.

    Property owners can purchase Bar Codes and Fobs at the Access Control office in the Clubhouse. Gate codes are available at no cost to residents.

    Renters must submit proof of leasing agreement to purchase access devices

    Pristine Place is a private, gated community dedicated to ensuring the security, privacy, and safety of our residents. Entry to our neighborhood is controlled through a sophisticated system of barcodes, key fobs, and 3-digit Gate Codes for the callbox.

    Barcode: A barcode is an adhesive sticker placed on the exterior of a vehicle’s passenger-side window. It allows entry through any of Pristine Place’s three gates when scanned by a barcode reader.

    Key Fob: A key fob is a compact (approximately 2-inch) device attached to a key ring. It grants access to the clubhouse, pool/exercise area, and specific gates. Simply hold the fob near the scanning device at the door or gate to unlock it.

    Gate Code: A 3-digit Gate Code is assigned to residents for use at the callbox. Visitors enter this code to contact a resident, who can then grant entry remotely.

    Access credentials—barcodes, key fobs, and Gate Codes—are issued exclusively to property owners and verified renters, ensuring that only authorized individuals can enter the community.
